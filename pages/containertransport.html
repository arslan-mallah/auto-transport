Frequently Asked Questions

Containers are being loaded on trailers with the help of special hauling equipment, Forklifts, cranes, driverless AGVs and top pickers are being used to lift and position them properly in trailers for secure and reliable transportation.

The type of trailer to use for shipping a container depends on different factors, but most importantly it depends on whether it’s a fully loaded container or an empty one. Different trailers are being used as per shipping requirements to transport a container. for an empty container, a land doll or tilt bed trailer is considered the most effective and economical option but in the case of a loaded container; special consideration and handling is required as per the circumstances.  a Skelton trailer (container chassis); two two-wheel-axle is mostly used for shipping of 20-foot and 40-foot standard containers.  A tilt container is a good option too, it provides ease in loading and unloading of the container.
