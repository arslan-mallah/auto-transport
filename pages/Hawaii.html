How To Prepare Your Car for Shipping

There are a few considerations and a checklist you should review to get your vehicle ready for shipment. A vehicle should be in running condition, with valid registration and a license plate to be eligible for shipment. We've created a detailed checklist you should follow before transporting your vehicle to speed up your shipment process.

Gas Tank Guidelines

Before shipping your vehicle, make sure the gas tank is at least half full that will be enough for the vehicle to reach a nearby gas station in Hawaii. If you are shipping an electric vehicle, ensure the battery charge is at least 45% to 50%.

Get Vehicle Photos

Many people don't take a photo of their vehicle's condition, which could provide important proof and ensure insurance claims in case of damage during shipping. Always take proof of your vehicle's condition before shipment.

Remove Additional Items

During shipment, all personal items and additional equipment inside the car cannot be shipped with it. Make sure to remove all unnecessary items, leaving only permanently installed items in the car.

Documentation

The shipping vehicle's owner must have a proper driving license, vehicle documents proving ownership details, and also a photo ID.

Vehicle Condition

Make sure the vehicle being shipped does not have major mechanical issues or fluid leaks. At the time of shipment, the vehicle should be in clean condition.
